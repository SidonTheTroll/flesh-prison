# Equilibrium of Forces 
- **Equilibrium:** if the resultant of a number of forces, acting on a particle is zero, the particle will be in equilibrium. Such a set of forces whose resultant is zero are called equilibrium forces. 
- **FBD (free body diagram):** a free body diagram is a sketch of a isolated object showing all the external forces acting on it, represented by arrows with their direction and magnitude. Key points:
    1. The object is isolated from its surroundings 
    2. All forces acting on the object are shown 
    3. Forces are represented by arrows pointing in the direction of the force 
    4. It helps in analyzing motion and solving mechanics problems using newtong's laws

# Equilibrant 
The force which brings a set of forces in equilibrium is called an equilibrant. 

## Relation between Equilibrant and Resultant 
The equilibrant is equal to the resultant force in magnitude but opposite in direction.
