# Interconversion of Number Systems 
Number System is a method of representing quantities by using digits and a base.  
It is used in digital electronics and computers because digital circuits operate naturally by using binary signals 0 and 1 while humans find decimal convenient

- Decimal: 10 nos. = (0-9)
- Octal: 8 nos. = (0-7)
- Hexadecimal: 16 nos. = (0-9),(A-F) 
- Binary: 2 nos. = (0,1)

## Decimal to Binary 
Decimal to binary- to convert decimal to binary, repeated division by 2 is performed, and the remainders are recorded. The binary equivalent is obtained by reading the numbers from bottom to top. This method works because each remainder represents the coefficient of power of 2.
