- **Experiment no:** 06 
- **Name of the experiment:** Transformation Ratio Test on a Single Phase Transformer. 
- **Object of the experiment:** To find the voltage at secondary of a single phase transformer with varying voltage at primary side and to find the voltage ratio. 

## Theory
A transformer transfers electrical energy between two circuits by electromagnetic induction. When AC voltage is applied to the primary coil, a changing magnetic flux induces a voltage in the secondary coil, with the transformation ratio defined as:

$$
\frac{V_2}{V_1} = \frac{N_2}{N_1} = k
$$

In a step-down transformer, the secondary voltage is lower than the primary, where $k < 1$. This experiment verifies the transformation ratio by measuring primary and secondary voltages.
