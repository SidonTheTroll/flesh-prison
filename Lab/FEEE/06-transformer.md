- **Experiment no:** 06 
- **Name of the experiment:** Transformation Ratio Test on a Single Phase Transformer. 
- **Object of the experiment:** To find the voltage at secondary of a single phase transformer with varying voltage at primary side and to find the voltage ratio. 

## Circuit Diagram 

## Procedure 
1. The circuit is made as shown in the figure above auto-transformer is connected to the primary side. So that the input of the transformer can be varied. 
2. The voltmeter $(V_1)$ is also connected to the primary side, i.e., in the secondary side, a voltmeter $(V_2)$ is connected which gives the secondary voltage $(E_2)$. 
3. Then the ratio between the primary voltage $(E_1)$ and secondary voltage $(E_2)$ is obtained as shown in the table below.
