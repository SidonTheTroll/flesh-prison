# Identification of Electronic Components

1. **What is a diode?**  
   **Answer:** A diode is an electronic component that allows current to flow in only one direction.

2. **What are the two terminals of a diode?**  
   **Answer:** Anode and Cathode.

3. **What is the function of a diode?**  
   **Answer:** To permit current flow in one direction and block it in the opposite direction.

4. **What is a Zener diode?**  
   **Answer:** A special diode designed to operate in the reverse breakdown region at a specified voltage.

5. **What is the main application of a Zener diode?**  
   **Answer:** Voltage regulation.

6. **What is the Zener voltage?**  
   **Answer:** The reverse voltage at which a Zener diode starts conducting.

7. **What is a breadboard?**  
   **Answer:** A board used for building and testing electronic circuits without soldering.

8. **Why is a breadboard used?**  
   **Answer:** To create temporary circuits easily and safely.

9. **What is a resistor?**  
   **Answer:** A passive electronic component that opposes the flow of electric current.

10. **What is the function of a resistor?**  
    **Answer:** To limit or control current in a circuit.

11. **What is the SI unit of resistance?**  
    **Answer:** Ohm (Ω).

12. **What is resistor tolerance?**  
    **Answer:** The permissible variation in the actual resistance value from its rated value.

13. **What does the gold color band indicate in a resistor?**  
    **Answer:** ±5% tolerance.

14. **What does the silver color band indicate in a resistor?**  
    **Answer:** ±10% tolerance.

15. **What is resistor color coding?**  
    **Answer:** A system of colored bands used to indicate the resistance value and tolerance.

16. **What is a capacitor?**  
    **Answer:** An electronic component that stores electrical energy in an electric field.

17. **What are the main parts of a capacitor?**  
    **Answer:** Two conducting plates separated by a dielectric material.

18. **What is the function of a capacitor?**  
    **Answer:** To store and release electrical energy.

19. **What is the SI unit of capacitance?**  
    **Answer:** Farad (F).

20. **What is a dielectric?**  
    **Answer:** An insulating material placed between the plates of a capacitor.

21. **What is a digital multimeter?**  
    **Answer:** An electronic instrument used to measure voltage, current, and resistance.

22. **What quantities can a digital multimeter measure?**  
    **Answer:** Voltage, current, resistance, and continuity.

23. **What does LED stand for?**  
    **Answer:** Light Emitting Diode.

24. **What is an LED?**  
    **Answer:** A semiconductor device that emits light when current passes through it.

25. **What is the advantage of LEDs over ordinary lamps?**  
    **Answer:** Lower power consumption and longer life.

26. **What is a transistor?**  
    **Answer:** A semiconductor device used as a switch or amplifier.

27. **What are the main terminals of a transistor?**  
    **Answer:** Emitter, Base, and Collector.

28. **What is the function of a transistor?**  
    **Answer:** To amplify signals or act as an electronic switch.

29. **What is an inductor?**  
    **Answer:** An electronic component that stores energy in a magnetic field.

30. **What is the function of an inductor?**  
    **Answer:** To oppose changes in current.

31. **What is the SI unit of inductance?**  
    **Answer:** Henry (H).

32. **How does an inductor store energy?**  
    **Answer:** In the form of a magnetic field.

33. **What is an Integrated Circuit (IC)?**  
    **Answer:** A miniature electronic circuit fabricated on a semiconductor chip.

34. **What are the components commonly found inside an IC?**  
    **Answer:** Transistors, resistors, capacitors, and diodes.

35. **What material is commonly used to manufacture ICs?**  
    **Answer:** Silicon.

36. **What is the advantage of an IC?**  
    **Answer:** Small size, low power consumption, and high reliability.

37. **What is a transformer?**  
    **Answer:** A static electrical device that transfers electrical energy through electromagnetic induction.

38. **What is the function of a transformer?**  
    **Answer:** To increase or decrease AC voltage levels.

39. **What is a step-up transformer?**  
    **Answer:** A transformer that increases voltage.

40. **What is a step-down transformer?**  
    **Answer:** A transformer that decreases voltage.

41. **Can a transformer operate on DC supply?**  
    **Answer:** No, a transformer requires AC supply.

42. **What is electromagnetic induction?**  
    **Answer:** The production of EMF due to a changing magnetic field.

43. **What is the function of wires and cables?**  
    **Answer:** To carry electrical current from one point to another.

44. **What is the difference between a wire and a cable?**  
    **Answer:** A wire is a single conductor, while a cable consists of two or more insulated conductors.

45. **Why is insulation provided on wires and cables?**  
    **Answer:** To prevent leakage current and electrical shock.

46. **Which electronic component stores energy in an electric field?**  
    **Answer:** Capacitor.

47. **Which electronic component stores energy in a magnetic field?**  
    **Answer:** Inductor.

48. **Which electronic component emits light when energized?**  
    **Answer:** LED.

49. **Which electronic component is primarily used for amplification?**  
    **Answer:** Transistor.

50. **What is the importance of identifying electronic components?**  
    **Answer:** It helps in circuit construction, testing, troubleshooting, and maintenance.
